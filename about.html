Malik is the founder and lead instructor of Nighters Cyber Academy. His goal is simple: to teach cybersecurity in a way that is easy to understand, practical, and accessible to everyone — even if you are starting with just a phone and a data connection.

This academy was built for students who are serious about learning. Not theory. Not slides. Real tools. Real commands. Real problems. The curriculum covers everything from basic Linux to full red team operations — step by step, at your own pace.
