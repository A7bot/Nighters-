Mission Brief
Nighters Cyber Academy is a structured Red Team training program.
3 months. 30 levels. 50% web hacking. 50% system hacking.
Tools: Termux + Debian on your phone. No expensive laptop needed for Phase 1.
All training is on authorized systems only. Read the rules before starting.

Linux basics · Networking · Bash scripting · OSINT · Google Dorking. Works on phone (Termux + Debian).
