Rules of Engagement
Read carefully. Ignorance is not an excuse.
All rules apply to every student, every phase, every day.
General Conduct
1. Authorized Use Only

All training targets are provided by the academy. You must NEVER attack systems you do not have explicit permission to attack. Unauthorized scanning or exploitation outside of designated lab environments is a criminal offense.

2. Respect All Members

Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language toward any student or instructor will result in immediate suspension. The academy is a professional environment.

3. No Sharing of Flags or Answers

Sharing flags, solutions, or exam answers — in any form — is cheating. This includes DMs, group chats, screenshots, or public posts. First offense: Strike 2. Second: permanent removal.

4. No Fake Submissions

Submitting flags you did not find yourself, brute-forcing the flag submission system, or attempting to manipulate your score will result in immediate removal and a ban.

Strike System
Strike 1 — Warning

Official warning issued. Your record is flagged. No immediate penalty.

Strike 2 — Leaderboard Penalty

Points deducted at admin discretion. You remain in the program but are under close monitoring.

Strike 3 — Removal

Permanent removal from the academy. Account disabled. No appeal.

Academic Integrity
The Spirit of the Academy

This academy exists to make you a better security professional. Cheating only hurts you — you leave without the skills you came for. We track everything not to catch you, but to ensure fairness for the students who put in the real work. The leaderboard represents genuine effort. Protect that integrity.

Learn through struggle. The hard challenges are the most valuable.
Ask for help from instructors if you are stuck — that is what they are here for.
Hints cost points for a reason. Use them wisely.
