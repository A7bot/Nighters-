Rules of Engagement

Read all rules before starting. Breaking any rule leads to strikes. Three strikes means permanent removal.

Core Rules
1. Authorized Systems Only

Only test systems you have written permission to test. Never scan or attack systems that do not belong to you. This is a serious crime in most countries.

2. No Flag or Answer Sharing

Do not share flags, answers, or solutions with anyone — not in private messages, not in group chats, not in any form. First offense is Strike 2.

3. No Cheating on Exams

Exams are individual. No discussing questions during exam window. Detected cheating = immediate Strike 3 and removal.

4. Respect Every Member

No harassment. No insults. No discrimination. This is a professional environment. One violation leads to Strike 2.

Strike System
Strike 1 — Warning

Official warning. Your record is flagged. No immediate punishment. Consider this your last chance.

Strike 2 — Points Penalty

Admin deducts points from your score. You remain in the program under close monitoring.

Strike 3 — Permanent Removal

Account disabled. Removed from leaderboard. No appeal. No return.

Learning Ethics
The Right Way to Think

This academy teaches you to think like an attacker. That knowledge is powerful. Use it ethically. The goal is to make systems more secure — not to cause harm. Every technique you learn here has a legitimate professional use in penetration testing, security auditing, and defensive security.

Authorized penetration testing is a legal profession. Unauthorized hacking is a crime. The only difference is permission. Always get written authorization before testing anything.
