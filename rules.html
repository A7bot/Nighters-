Rules of Engagement

Read all system operational mandates before initializing protocols. Breaking deployment criteria yields explicit infraction strikes. Three cumulative strikes trigger permanent system execution revocation.

Core Rules
1. Authorized Target Infrastructure Only

Only perform diagnostic testing arrays against environments you hold documented scope and permission maps for. Unauthorized scans or exploitation vectors constitute legal infractions globally.

2. Direct Prohibition of Solution/Flag Sharing

Do not broadcast cryptographic flags, data payloads, or target step-by-step solutions to other candidates via direct vectors, open channels, or logs. First verified event is escalated immediately to Strike 2.

3. Assessment Isolation Controls

All milestone verification exams remain individual operational assessments. Collective collaboration loops during testing availability frames are logged. Detections result in instant Strike 3 terminal termination.

4. Maintain Professional Operational Protocol

Harassment arrays, toxicity vectors, or explicit disruption attempts targeting other operants will not be tolerated inside academy clusters. Violations trigger an instantaneous Strike 2 assignment.

Strike System
Strike 1 — Formal Flag Warning

The administrative database notes an anomalous activity marker. No tactical penalties are currently levied. Treat this alert threshold as your final grace state indicator.

Strike 2 — Core Score Points Penalty

Administrative systems enforce a score value adjustment penalty. Profile telemetry is assigned a restricted, heavily-audited inspection filter for all subsequent actions.

Strike 3 — Definitive Account Exclusion

The target account footprint is permanently disabled. Dynamic global tracking records are wiped. No arbitration, rollback, or secondary registry attempts are permitted.
